WEATHER APP

My weather application is a user-friendly tool crafted with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, providing current weather updates and five-day forecasts through seamless integration with weather APIs. Offering essential details like temperature, humidity percentage, and wind speed, alongside the current date and time, the application delivers accurate and timely weather information in a sleek and intuitive interface. Whether planning for the day or the week ahead, users can rely on this minimalist-designed application to stay informed and make informed decisions based on the forecast.
